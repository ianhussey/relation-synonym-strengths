Consent Form

Overview
The purpose of this study is to examine the of synonyms (words that mean the same thing). You will categorize words under time pressure.

Your decision to participate in this research is entirely voluntary. You can withdraw from the study at any time without penalty. If you have any questions or issues with this study please contact the researcher: ian.hussey@ugent.be

Requirements
Normal or correct-to-normal vision (e.g., wearing glasses or contact lenses).

Risks
This study involves no known risks to your wellbeing.

Compensation
You will receive a sum of money for participating, as detailed on the Prolific website. Please note that low quality data (e.g., non-genuine or automated responses) will be checked for via an algorithm. Low quality or incomplete sessions may have payment rejected.

Confidentiality and data usage
All data will be irrevocably anonymised as soon as data collection is completed. Later deletion of your individual data is therefore not possible. Your data will be aggregated with others, analysed and may be written up as part of an academic manuscript. Your anonymised data may also be made available on a public repository. The purpose and scope of subsequent use of your data is not forseeable.
